Yu Sun

Email: yusun [at] cs.stanford.edu

I work in artificial intelligence and machine learning. My current research direction is training a different model on-the-fly for each test instance. I call this test-time training.

I am a postdoc at Stanford University, hosted by Carlos Guestrin, Tatsu Hashimoto, and Sanmi Koyejo.

I completed my PhD in EECS at UC Berkeley, advised by Alyosha Efros and Moritz Hardt. Previously, I was an undergraduate student at Cornell University, where I worked with Kilian Weinberger.

For a complete list of papers, please see my Google Scholar.
